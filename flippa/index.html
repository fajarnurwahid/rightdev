Operations

The business requires little supervision (30 minutes a week), with most of the heavy lifting done by subcontractors. Currently, there are 6 contractors and 2 agencies, and most of the work is outsourced to them.

Our product line is sold directly to consumers, with most sold to domestic customers and a healthy list of engaged and repeat buyers. The products are sold 100% on the Shopify platform, with traffic driven from SEO, Facebook, Instagram & Google Advertising.

When a customer places an order on our website, our 3PL partner fulfils and ships the order from their location in the US. We offer 2-5 day shipping or even 1 day express shipping.
